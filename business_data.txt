ABOUT:
Ambika Padavi Poorva Vidyalaya was founded in 2009 in Puttur, Dakshina Kannada.
Established by Nattoja Foundation Trust under leadership of 
Shri Subramanya Nattoja and Smt. Rajashree Nattoja.
Rated 5.0/5 by students.
The college has two campuses:
1. Nellikatte Campus - Day Scholar Campus
2. Bappalige Campus - Residential Campus

VISION:
To be the preeminent teaching college linking people of 
Karnataka to the nation and the world.

MISSION:
To train young minds in the correct path so that younger 
generation becomes self confident and capable of facing 
any practical challenge in life.

COMMON QUESTIONS:

Q: What courses are available after 10th standard?
A: We offer 1st and 2nd PUC in Science and Commerce 
streams with competitive exam coaching included.

Q: Do you provide IIT JEE coaching?
A: Yes! We provide K-CET, JEE Mains and Advanced, 
NEET, NATA and NDA coaching along with regular PUC.

Q: Is hostel facility available?
A: Yes! We have SEPARATE hostels for Boys and Girls 
at our Bappalige Residential Campus. Hostel is not 
available at Nellikatte campus which is day scholar only.

Q: Are scholarships available?
A: Yes! Ambika offers scholarships to meritorious 
students based on periodic exam performance. 
This is unique to Ambika college.

Q: What is the admission process?
A: Collect application form from college office after 
SSLC results or download from website. Submit with 
required documents and registration fees.

Q: Are yoga classes available?
A: Yes! Free yoga classes are provided to all students.

Q: Is NEET coaching available?
A: Yes! NEET coaching is available including a special 
NEET Long Term Batch for serious aspirants.

Q: Which campus should I choose?
A: If you need hostel facility, choose Bappalige 
Residential Campus. If you are a local day scholar, 
choose Nellikatte Campus.

Q: What is the fee structure?
A: Fees are moderate and affordable for all students. 
For exact fee details please contact us directly at 
+91 94488 35488 (Nellikatte) or +91 87628 92488 (Bappalige).

Q: What subjects are available in Science stream?
A: Science stream offers PCMB (Physics, Chemistry, 
Maths, Biology) and PCMCs (Physics, Chemistry, 
Maths, Computer Science).

Q: What competitive exams do you coach for?
A: We coach for K-CET, JEE Mains, JEE Advanced, 
NEET, NATA, NDA, CA, and CLAT.
